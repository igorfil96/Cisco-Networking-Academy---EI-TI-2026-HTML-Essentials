Kyoto: The Heartbeat of Ancient Japan ⛩️

My first morning in Kyoto felt like stepping into a watercolor painting. I remember the sharp, clean scent of cedarwood as I walked through the gates of a hidden temple in Higashiyama. Unlike the neon-soaked energy of Tokyo, Kyoto whispered to me through the sound of gravel crunching underfoot and the distant chime of a meditation bell.
